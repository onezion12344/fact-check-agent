# OneDrive Subscription Expiry Policy

> Verified 2026-06-26 against multiple Microsoft official sources.

## Official Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Can Do | Cannot Do |
|:------|:---------|:-------|:-----------|
| **Expired** | 30 days | View, edit, download files | — |
| **Disabled/Frozen** | ~90 days | View & download only **read-only** | Upload, edit, sync |
| **Deleted** | After ~6 months total | — | — |

Source: Microsoft Support page "What does it mean when your OneDrive account is frozen" + Microsoft Learn subscription lifecycle.

## Key Facts

- Storage reduces from 1TB to **5GB** (free tier) immediately
- Files over 5GB are **NOT deleted immediately** — they become read-only
- After **6 months** of inactivity, Microsoft **MAY** delete all files
- Reactivation: Pay and renew → full access restored immediately
- This applies to Microsoft 365 Personal and Family subscriptions

## Comparison with Other Providers

| Platform | Expiry Action | Grace Period | Deletion Timeline |
|:---------|:-------------|:-------------|:-----------------|
| OneDrive | Read-only, no upload | ~120 days total | 6 months |
| Google One | Read-only, no upload | Unlimited grace | Not deleted (just locked) |
| iCloud | Read-only, no sync | Varies | May delete immediately |
| Tencent COS | Service stops on debt | 24h inaccessible | 30 days to destroy |
