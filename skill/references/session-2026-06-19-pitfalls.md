## Key Lessons

1. **GPU/numeric data**: Always pull from GSMArena/Notebookcheck directly. Never trust memory or SEO spam sites.
2. **Prices**: Open the product page. Search snippets are wrong 80% of the time.
3. **Accessories specs**: Check the manufacturer's product page, not just Amazon titles.
4. **SIM specs**: GSMArena lists exact SIM configurations. Don't guess.
5. **Battery**: GSMArena spec pages are authoritative. User's own phone might differ from your memory.
6. **Parallel sub-agents for fact-checking work**: 3 agents doing domain-chunked cross-verification catches more errors than sequential checks.
